The African Genetic Biocontrol Consortium is delighted to announce the 1st Global Congress on New and Emerging Genetic Biocontrol Technologies to take place in Nairobi, Kenya in August 28 – Sept 02, 2023. The aim of the Congress is to bring together experts from around the world to share learnings across diverse disciplines related to genetic biocontrol technologies in a six-day event at the Safari Park Hotel.

Read More

About the Congress

The Congress is a premier event that brings together experts in genetic biocontrol to discuss the latest advances and innovations in the field. The event is characterized by:

  • Three full days of intensive Pre-Conference Workshops/Symposia/Courses
  • Three full days of state-of-the-art keynotes, papers, and panel discussions
  • Exhibits showcasing the latest genetic biocontrol products and services
  • Invaluable networking opportunities

Topics For Consideration

The following topics are applicable to genetic biocontrol agents and/or products and can be used as a guide to develop proposals for Pre-Conference Courses/Workshops/Symposia as well as abstracts for presentations during the Conference:

  • Genetics and genetic engineering tools
  • Public health
  • Agriculture, conservation, livestock, and wildlife health
  • Approaches and disease-carrying insects
  • Biosafety, biosecurity, and biocontainment
  • Stakeholder and public engagement
  • Quality control and certification of facilities
  • One health
  • Training and capacity building
  • Policy and regulatory science of field trials and releases
  • Classical biocontrol
  • Non-genetic/Wolbachia studies
  • Mathematical modeling
  • Risk assessment and decision-making
  • Science communication
  • Leadership, governance, and related management issues
  • Innovation, stewardship, entrepreneurship, and related topics
  • Product registration and labeling of GM products

Call for Abstracts

Join us for the 1st Global Congress on Emerging Genetic Biocontrol Technologies 2023 and be part of the globe’s premier Congress to advance the Genetic Biocontrol agenda.

This is an excellent opportunity to display and discuss your products and services to an international audience. Anyone who supplies, manufactures, provides services or products used in biomedical research, pest and disease control, food and feed products, biosafety and biosecurity should exhibit.
